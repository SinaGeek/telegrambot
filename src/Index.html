📁 What can I do:

1️⃣ I can upload any telegram files like Video, Document, Photo, Audio, Voice, GIFs to your Google Drive.

2️⃣ I can upload files from Direct Download Links too.

🔗 How to link Google Drive:

↗️ Get your sign-in link from /login.

↗️ Go to the login page, Sign in with your Google account, allow the requested access, and that's it 🤠.

You can re-connect anytime using /login.

🚀 How to use the bot:

↘️ Upload: Send a document/photo to upload it to your Drive.

↘️ List: Use /list to see recent files (with IDs).

↘️ Rename: /rename <fileId> <newName>

↘️ Remove: /remove <fileId>

⚙️ Commands

/start — Check bot status
/help — Show commands
/login — Connect your Google Drive
/list — List recent files
/rename <fileId> <newName> — Rename file
/remove <fileId> — Delete file
/privacy — Privacy policy and Terms
