🔗 How to link Google Drive:

↗️ Get your Sign In link from /log_in.

↗️ Go to the login page, Sign in with your Google account, allow the requested access, and that's it 🤠.

You can add multiple Google Drive accounts with the bot. Use the above method for the same.

To switch between your saved accounts use /mygdrives.

To logout from current Google Drive account use /log_out.

↘️ Uploading Telegram Files: To upload telegram files, just forward the telegram file.

↘️ Uploading from Links: Send me any direct download links that are not IP specific. You can set custom file names for your files.
